The [Black Hat](https://www.blackhat.com) event network has been called [one of the most hostile in the world](https://www.darkreading.com/perimeter/securing-black-hat-from-black-hat). At [Palo Alto Networks](https://www.paloaltonetworks.com), we've been involved in operating and securing this network since 2017. In 2025, we decided to share a behind the scenes insight into what it's like working, around the clock, to keep one of the world's most infamous network secure.
